## Who

Jamie Curle is a technologist, engineer and leader based in Northumberland, UK.
He has over twenty five years of experience across the full stack, with deep 
expertise in engineering, engineering leadership, privacy, information security 
and AI governance, held together by a foundation in design.

He has held IC, EM and executive roles (CTO and CIO) and does not fit neatly 
into one box. That is a feature of his profile, not a gap in it. He is currently 
open to the right opportunity across any of those levels.

He is also a qualified arborist and active woodland manager. This is not a 
quirky footnote — it is part of how he thinks and operates.
